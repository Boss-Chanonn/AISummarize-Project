What Pro unlocks
The premium layer should read like a natural extension of the existing product, so each capability is shown in the same restrained card language used elsewhere in Learnova.
Pro
PPTX / PowerPoint upload
Upload presentation files. AI summarises every slide once and saves the full structured summary — so you can return to study any slide range any time, with no re-processing needed.
Pro
Multi-file batch upload
Upload up to 5 documents at once. Each file gets its own AI summary and quiz. Ideal for studying multiple papers or chapters in a single session.
Pro
Advanced personalised feedback
After each quiz, get deep AI analysis of your answer patterns — not just right or wrong, but why you got each answer wrong and what concepts to revisit.
Pro
Weekly progress summary
Every week, get an AI-generated summary of your study activity — documents covered, average score trend, topics you're improving in, and what to focus on next week.
Pro
Full learning modules
Access curated learning resources tied to each document — videos, articles, podcasts — and schedule them into your calendar. Free users see the module but cannot schedule.
Pro
Calendar integration
Schedule study sessions directly into Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook from within the learning module. Stay on track without switching apps.
